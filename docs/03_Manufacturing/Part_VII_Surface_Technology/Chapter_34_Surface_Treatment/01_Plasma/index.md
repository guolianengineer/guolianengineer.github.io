
# Plasma Treatment
**等离子处理**

A surface modification technique that uses ionized gas (plasma) to clean, activate, or coat surfaces.
> 一种使用电离气体（等离子体）来清洁、活化或涂覆表面的表面改性技术。

---

## 1. Types (类型)
*   **Atmospheric Plasma (常压等离子)**: Uses a nozzle to spray plasma jet. Suitable for inline processing of specific areas.
    *   使用喷嘴喷射等离子流。适用于特定区域的在线处理。
*   **Vacuum Plasma (真空等离子)**: Parts are placed in a vacuum chamber. Suitable for batch processing of complex 3D shapes.
    *   零件放置在真空腔体中。适用于复杂 3D 形状的批量处理。

## 2. Mechanism (机理)
*   **Cleaning (清洁)**: Removes organic contaminants (oils, release agents) at the molecular level.
    *   在分子水平上去除有机污染物（油污、脱模剂）。
*   **Activation (活化)**: Increases surface energy (dyne level) by introducing polar groups (e.g., -OH, -COOH).
    *   通过引入极性基团（如 -OH, -COOH）提高表面能（达因值）。

## 3. Applications (应用)
*   **Pre-bonding (粘接前处理)**: Essential for bonding low-surface-energy plastics like PP, PE.
    *   对于粘接 PP、PE 等低表面能塑料至关重要。
*   **Pre-printing (印刷前处理)**: Ensures ink adhesion on glass or metal.
    *   确保油墨在玻璃或金属上的附着力。

## 4. Validation (验证)
*   **Dyne Pen Test (达因笔测试)**: Quick check. The ink should spread evenly without beading up. Standard: > 38 dyne/cm.
    *   快速检查。墨水应均匀铺展而不聚珠。标准：> 38 dyne/cm。
*   **Water Contact Angle (水滴角测试)**: Lab measurement. Lower angle = Higher surface energy.
    *   实验室测量。角度越小 = 表面能越高。

## 5. Critical Considerations (关键考量)
*   **Aging Effect (时效效应)**: The activation effect decays over time. Bonding should be done within a specific time window (e.g., 24 hours).
    *   活化效果随时间衰减。粘接应在特定时间窗口内完成（例如 24 小时）。
