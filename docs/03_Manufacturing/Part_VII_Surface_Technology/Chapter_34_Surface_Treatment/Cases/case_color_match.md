
# Case Study: Anodizing Color Matching for Space Grey
**案例：深空灰阳极氧化色差管控**

## Problem (问题)
High rejection rate (15%) due to color variation in "Space Grey" anodized parts.
> 由于“深空灰”阳极氧化零件的颜色变异，导致拒收率高达 15%。

## Analysis (分析)
*   **Bath Parameter**: Temperature fluctuation was +/- 2°C (Standard: +/- 1°C).
    *   槽液温度波动为 +/- 2°C（标准：+/- 1°C）。
*   **Dye Concentration**: Manual replenishment caused inconsistency.
    *   人工补料导致染料浓度不一致。

## Action (对策)
*   Installed automated dosing system for dye.
    *   安装了染料自动添加系统。
*   Upgraded chiller to control temperature within +/- 0.5°C.
    *   升级了冷水机，将温度控制在 +/- 0.5°C 以内。
