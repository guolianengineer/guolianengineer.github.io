
# Surface Treatments (Chapter 34)
**表面处理（第34章）**

Surface treatment is a process applied to the surface of a material to make it better in some way, for example by making it more resistant to corrosion or wear, or improving its appearance.
> 表面处理是应用于材料表面的工艺，旨在以某种方式改善其性能，例如提高耐腐蚀性或耐磨性，或改善其外观。

## 1. Anodizing (阳极氧化)

An electrochemical process that converts the metal surface into a decorative, durable, corrosion-resistant, anodic oxide finish. Commonly used for Aluminum.
> 一种电化学过程，将金属表面转化为装饰性、耐用、耐腐蚀的阳极氧化膜。通常用于铝合金。

### Types (类型)
*   **Type II (Decorative)**: Thinner layer, easy to dye colors.
    *   II 型（装饰性）：膜层较薄，易于染色。
*   **Type III (Hard)**: Thicker, harder layer, excellent wear resistance.
    *   III 型（硬质）：膜层较厚、较硬，耐磨性极佳。

### Key Parameters (关键参数)
*   **Bath Temperature (槽液温度)**
*   **Current Density (电流密度)**
*   **Time (时间)**

## 2. PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition)
**物理气相沉积**

A vacuum coating process that produces a brilliant decorative and functional finish.
> 一种真空镀膜工艺，可产生光亮、装饰性和功能性的涂层。

*   **Applications (应用)**: Smartphone frames, watch cases, cutting tools.
    *   智能手机边框、手表外壳、切削刀具。
*   **Advantages (优点)**: High hardness, scratch resistance, metallic colors.
    *   高硬度、耐刮擦、金属质感颜色。

---

## 3. Sandblasting (喷砂)

The operation of forcibly propelling a stream of abrasive material against a surface under high pressure to smooth a rough surface or roughen a smooth surface.
> 在高压下强制推动磨料流撞击表面，以平滑粗糙表面或粗化光滑表面的操作。

*   **Media (磨料)**: Glass beads, ceramic beads, aluminum oxide.
    *   玻璃珠、陶瓷珠、氧化铝。
*   **Effect (效果)**: Matte finish, hides machining marks.
    *   哑光效果，遮盖加工刀纹。

---

## 4. Painting & Coating (喷涂)

*   **Liquid Painting (液体喷涂)**: PU, UV paints. High gloss or soft-touch effects.
    *   PU漆、UV漆。高光或触感效果。
*   **Powder Coating (粉末喷涂)**: Dry powder applied electrostatically and cured under heat. Thicker and more durable.
    *   静电喷涂干粉并在热作用下固化。更厚、更耐用。
