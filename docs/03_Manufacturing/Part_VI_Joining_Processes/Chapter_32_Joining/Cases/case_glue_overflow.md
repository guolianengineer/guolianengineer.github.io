
# Case Study: Preventing Glue Overflow in Lens Bonding
**案例：防止镜片粘接溢胶**

## Issue (问题)
Glue overflowed onto the visible surface of the camera lens during assembly.
> 组装过程中，胶水溢出到了相机镜片的可视表面。

## Solution (解决方案)
1.  **Design Change**: Added a "glue trench" (overflow channel) to the housing design.
    *   设计变更：在外壳设计中增加了“溢胶槽”。
2.  **Process Optimization**: Reduced dispensing volume by 10% and increased robot speed.
    *   工艺优化：减少 10% 的点胶量并提高了机器人速度。
