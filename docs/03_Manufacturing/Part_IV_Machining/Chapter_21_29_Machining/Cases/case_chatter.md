
# Case Study: Eliminating Chatter on Aluminum Enclosure
**案例：消除铝合金外壳的铣削颤纹**

## Background (背景)
During the CNC milling of a thin-walled aluminum enclosure, severe chatter marks were observed on the side walls.
> 在铝合金薄壁外壳的 CNC 铣削过程中，侧壁出现了严重的颤纹。

## Root Cause Analysis (根本原因分析)
1.  **Tool Stick-out (刀具悬伸)**: The end mill stick-out was 45mm, too long for a 6mm diameter tool.
    *   铣刀悬伸长达 45mm，对于直径 6mm 的刀具来说太长了。
2.  **Fixture Rigidity (治具刚性)**: The clamping force was insufficient in the center area.
    *   治具中心区域的夹紧力不足。

## Solution (解决方案)
1.  **Optimized Toolpath (优化刀路)**: Used a "trochoidal milling" strategy to reduce cutting forces.
    *   采用“摆线铣”策略以降低切削力。
2.  **Fixture Improvement (治具改进)**: Added a support block in the center.
    *   在中心增加了支撑块。

## Result (结果)
Chatter marks eliminated. Surface finish improved from Ra 1.6 to Ra 0.4.
> 颤纹消除。表面粗糙度从 Ra 1.6 提升至 Ra 0.4。
