
# Manufacturing Technology
**制造技术**

Structure based on *Manufacturing Engineering and Technology* (Kalpakjian & Schmid).
> 本板块结构参考《制造工程与技术》（Kalpakjian & Schmid）教材。

## Part I: Fundamentals of Materials (材料基础)
*   **Chapter 1-9**: Properties of Metals, Polymers, Ceramics, Composites.
    *   金属、聚合物、陶瓷、复合材料的性能。

## Part II: Metal-casting Processes (铸造工艺)
*   **Chapter 10-12**: Sand casting, Die casting, Investment casting.
    *   砂型铸造、压铸、熔模铸造。

## Part III: Forming and Shaping (成形工艺)
*   **Chapter 13-16**: Rolling, Forging, Extrusion, Sheet-metal forming.
    *   轧制、锻造、挤压、板金成形。

## Part IV: Machining Processes (切削加工)
*   **[Chapter 21-29: Machining (切削加工)](./Part_IV_Machining/Chapter_21_29_Machining/index.md)**
    *   Turning, Milling, Drilling, CNC.
    *   车削、铣削、钻削、数控加工。

## Part V: Micromanufacturing (微纳制造)
*   **Chapter 28-29**: Microelectronic devices, MEMS, Nanofabrication.
    *   微电子器件、MEMS、纳米制造。

## Part VI: Joining Processes (连接工艺)
*   **Chapter 30-31**: Fusion welding, Solid-state welding.
    *   熔焊、固相焊。
*   **[Chapter 32: Brazing, Soldering, Adhesive-bonding (钎焊、胶接)](./Part_VI_Joining_Processes/Chapter_32_Joining/index.md)**
    *   Adhesive bonding, Mechanical fastening.
    *   胶接、机械紧固。

## Part VII: Surface Technology (表面技术)
*   **Chapter 33**: Surface roughness, Tribology.
    *   表面粗糙度、摩擦学。
*   **[Chapter 34: Surface Treatments (表面处理)](./Part_VII_Surface_Technology/Chapter_34_Surface_Treatment/index.md)**
    *   Anodizing, PVD, Painting, Cleaning (Plasma).
    *   阳极氧化、PVD、喷涂、清洁（等离子）。

## Part VIII: Metrology & Quality Assurance (计量与质量保证)
*   **Chapter 35**: Engineering Metrology (Dimensional measurement).
    *   工程计量（尺寸测量）。
*   **Chapter 36**: Quality Assurance, Testing, Inspection (SPC).
    *   质量保证、测试、检验（SPC）。
