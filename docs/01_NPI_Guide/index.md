
# New Product Introduction (NPI) Guide
**新产品导入指南**

The NPI phase is a critical process for a product from concept to mass production. As process engineers, we have specific deliverables at each stage.
> NPI 阶段是产品从概念到量产的关键过程。作为工艺工程师，我们在每个阶段都有特定的交付物。

---

## Stage Overview (阶段概览)

### 1. EVT (Engineering Validation Test)
**工程验证测试**

*   **Objective (目标)**: Verify design feasibility & Identify major process risks.
    *   验证设计可行性 & 识别主要工艺风险。
*   **Deliverables (交付物)**: DFM (Design for Manufacturing) Report.
    *   DFM（可制造性设计）报告。

### 2. DVT (Design Validation Test)
**设计验证测试**

*   **Objective**: Verify design maturity & Lock process parameters (DOE).
    *   验证设计成熟度 & 锁定工艺参数（DOE）。
*   **Key Actions (关键动作)**: Verify molds/fixtures.
    *   模具/治具验证。

### 3. PVT (Production Validation Test)
**生产验证测试**

*   **Objective**: Verify mass production capability & Yield Ramp.
    *   验证量产能力 & 良率爬坡。
*   **Focus (重点)**: CT (Cycle Time) optimization.
    *   生产节拍（CT）优化。

### 4. MP (Mass Production)
**量产**

*   **Focus**: Continuous improvement & Exception handling.
    *   持续改进 & 异常处理。
