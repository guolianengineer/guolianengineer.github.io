# New Product Introduction (NPI) Guide

The NPI phase is a critical process for a product from concept to mass production. As process engineers, we have specific deliverables at each stage.

## Stage Overview

1.  **EVT (Engineering Validation Test)**
    *   Verify design feasibility
    *   Identify major process risks
    *   DFM (Design for Manufacturing) report

2.  **DVT (Design Validation Test)**
    *   Verify design maturity
    *   Lock process parameters (DOE)
    *   Verify molds/fixtures

3.  **PVT (Production Validation Test)**
    *   Verify mass production capability
    *   Yield Ramp
    *   CT (Cycle Time) optimization

4.  **MP (Mass Production)**
    *   Continuous improvement
    *   Exception handling
